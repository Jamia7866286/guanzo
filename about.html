Brands portfolio

Established in 2016, Cy Cosmetics had been conceptualised to fill the gap of professional salon products market in India - initially by importing products from various countries but more recently, by establishing manufacturing processes with technology advanced machineries in India.

With “ Make in India”, initiative by Indian Prime Minister, as a core driver, we are committed to become largest shareholder in South Asian Market by 2025.

The man behind it all

Mr. Bikash Sharma is living in China since 1999. Being an entrepreneur by nature, he set up UTG group® to offer suite of services to customers across the globe but more importantly, connected Indians to the vast Chinese market.

Since then, UTG Group has served a multitude of customers across several industries to fulfil the supply chain demands and continues to deliver exceptional customer service.
